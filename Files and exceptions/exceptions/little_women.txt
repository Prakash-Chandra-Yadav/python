The four sisters gathered around the table on a cold winter evening.

Meg, the eldest, was practical and caring. Jo was energetic and full of imagination. Beth spoke softly and preferred music to conversation. Amy dreamed of becoming a great artist one day.

Although the family did not have much money, they shared laughter, kindness, and a strong bond with one another.

That evening, they discussed their hopes for the future. Each sister had a different dream, yet they encouraged one another and celebrated every small success.

As the snow fell outside, the sisters sat together by the fire. They read stories, played music, and talked about the adventures they hoped to experience.

Their mother reminded them that true happiness comes not from wealth but from love, hard work, and generosity toward others.

The sisters listened carefully. Though they would face many challenges in the years ahead, they knew they could rely on one another.

The warmth of the room and the comfort of family made the winter night feel peaceful and bright.
